## Data Cleaning


```python
df["Date"] = pd.to_datetime(df["Year"].astype(str)+"-12-31")
df["Attendance"] = df["Attendance"].str.replace(",","").astype(float)
df["Manager"] = df["Managers"].str.split("((\([0-9]))", expand = True)[0]
```
